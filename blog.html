A Day in Our School

Step into your child's joyful learning journey

7:30 AM – Arrival & Warm Welcome

Staff welcome children with warmth and a smile, ensuring a smooth start to their day.

8:00 AM – Assembly & Devotion

Our mornings begin with songs, prayers, and short inspiring lessons in moral values.

9:00 AM – Learning Blocks

Interactive lessons in Literacy, Numeracy, Science, and more, tailored to each class level.

10:30 AM – Snack & Free Play

Children enjoy their snacks and engage in free play to recharge and build friendships.

11:30 AM – Storytime & Exploration

Our little learners dive into imaginative stories and discovery-based activities.

12:30 PM – Creative Corners

Children express themselves through art, dance, music, and hands-on Montessori tasks.
