Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Clemmy Nursery and Primary School.

For all enquiries relating to admissions, you can do either of the following:

  1. Contact us directly on 08022236527, 08062375430, or 08177154335.
  2. Click here to fill an online admission form and come to the school with your child to pay for the form and write an entrance exam.

Please note that you must come to the school with the following:

  • Your child’s passport photographs (2)
  • Photocopy of your child’s birth certificate (1)
  • Last term’s result from previous school

Requirements

Age:
Children seeking admission to the following classes must have attained the class age by August 31st of the year of admission:
Early Years and Foundation Stage:
Crèche 1+ years
Preschool 1 2+ years
Preschool 2 3+ years
Reception 4+ years
