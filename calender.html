Enrichment Program

The school organizes curriculum enrichment programs such as field trips and healthy competitions. Our children visit interesting and educative places.

The competitions are geared toward developing the children's competitive skills, while rewarding their efforts. The competitions include:

  1. Essay Writing Competition.
  2. Spelling Bee Competition.
  3. Inter House Sports.
  4. Board Games Competition.
  5. Mathemania Competition.
  6. Literary and Debate Competition
  7. Chess Competition
