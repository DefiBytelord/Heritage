Curriculum

At Heritage Creativity School, we deliver a rich educational experience through a unique combination of the British National Curriculum and the Nigerian Curriculum. Our approach prepares learners to excel both locally and internationally.

Preschool / Nursery (Ages 2–5)

Our Early Years program caters to children aged 2 to 5 years. We align with the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) and draw inspiration from the Maria Montessori Method to nurture holistic development.

Learning is primarily play-based and covers the following key areas:

  • Communication
  • Physical Development
  • Personal, Social, and Emotional Development
  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • Understanding the World
  • Expressive Arts and Design

Primary School (Ages 5–11)

Our Primary School is structured into two stages:
Key Stage 1 (Ages 5–7) and Key Stage 2 (Ages 7–11)

The curriculum is designed to build a strong academic foundation and prepare learners for the Standard Attainment Tests (SATs) at the end of Years 2 and 6.

Core Subjects:

  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • Science
  • Computing (ICT)
  • Additional Languages(Chinese, French)
  • Art & Design
  • Music
  • History
  • Geography
  • Physical Education
  • Design & Technology

Nigerian Curriculum Integration

In line with our commitment to a balanced education, we integrate key elements of the Nigerian Curriculum into our teaching.

Subjects include:

  • Social Studies
  • Nigerian Languages (electives)
  • Geography

This blend ensures our students remain connected to their cultural heritage while meeting global educational standards.

Outdoor Learning

At Heritage Creativity School, we believe that outdoor learning enhances a child’s educational experience and overall development.

We are committed to creating exciting outdoor environments, including an outdoor classroom, vegetable gardens, and creative play spaces like willow tunnels. Teachers seamlessly weave outdoor activities into daily lessons, encouraging exploration, curiosity, and hands-on learning across subjects.

Sports & Extracurricular Activities

We offer a vibrant range of extracurricular clubs and sports opportunities for learners of all ages.

Our activities—ranging from arts, music, and dance to sports and science clubs—run before, during, and after school hours. A full list of available clubs is shared with parents at the start of each term.
